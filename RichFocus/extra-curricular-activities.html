Extra Curricular Activities

At RICHFOCUS SCHOOL, we also engage in EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

An extracurricular is any activity you participate in outside of class. It is incorporated into the school's activities. Extracurriculars cover a wide range of activities and interests, from painting to science to helping the homeless and more.

Below are the Extra Curricular Activties:

  • Taekwondo Training
  • Swimming
  • Ballet Dance
  • Digital Computer Learning

Some Advantages of Extracurricular Activities:

  • They Let You Do Something You Enjoy

    • Extracurriculars let you participate in an activity you enjoy. Practicing this activity regularly will help you get better at it, and you may be able to develop new skills that you find useful in the future. Doing something you enjoy not only makes you happier but can also give you a much-needed break from schoolwork.

  • They Introduce You To New Friends

    • Students often make many friends through their extracurriculars because they see other members regularly and have a shared interest.

  • They Are Important For College Applications

    • Extracurriculars can also be included in your college applications to show your interests and talents. This is because Colleges like to admit students who are involved in their communities, interact well with others, and work to develop their talents and passions. A student who participates in extracurriculars is more likely to do each of those things than a student who has no extracurriculars.
